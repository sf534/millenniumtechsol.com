Who We Are

A Technology Partner You Can Count On

Millennium Tech Solutions was founded with a single mission: to deliver enterprise-grade technology solutions with the personal touch of a community-focused partner. With over 20 years of experience in the technology industry — including extensive work across the Gulf region — we have brought that global expertise home to Southern California.

We specialize in IT equipment procurement, hardware solutions, VR/AR technology, and enterprise software. We are proud registered vendors with both Riverside County and Los Angeles County, and we actively pursue vendor and supplier relationships with major corporations, government agencies, and institutions throughout Southern California.

Our Journey

From the Gulf to Southern California

Two decades of building relationships, delivering quality, and growing with the communities we serve.

2004

Founded in the Gulf Region

Millennium Tech Solutions was established with a focus on IT equipment supply and technology services, quickly building a reputation for quality and reliability across the Gulf market.

2008–2015

Expanding Expertise & Client Base

Grew our portfolio to include enterprise hardware, networking infrastructure, and early-stage software solutions. Developed deep relationships with major technology manufacturers and distributors.

2016–2020

Embracing Emerging Technologies

Expanded into Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality solutions, positioning MTS as a forward-thinking technology partner capable of delivering next-generation enterprise applications.

2021

Establishing Roots in Southern California

Relocated and refocused operations to serve the Southern California market — Riverside County, LA County, and the broader Inland Empire — bringing 20 years of global expertise to the local community.

2023–Present

Registered Government Vendor & Growing

Successfully registered as a vendor with Riverside County and LA County for IT equipment and computer parts. Actively building relationships with major Southern California corporations and institutions.
