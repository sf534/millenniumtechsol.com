About Millennium Tech Solutions

Two decades of expertise, a passion for technology, and a commitment to the Southern California community.

Who We Are

A Technology Partner You Can Trust

Millennium Tech Solutions is a Southern California-based IT equipment supplier and technology services company dedicated to serving government agencies, enterprises, and businesses of all sizes. We bring a universal, professional approach to every engagement — combining deep technical expertise with genuine care for our clients and communities.

With over 20 years of experience in the technology industry — including extensive work serving clients in the Gulf region — our team has developed a broad, global perspective on IT challenges and solutions. Now firmly rooted in Southern California, we are channeling that expertise into serving Riverside County, Los Angeles County, and the broader SoCal business community.

Our mission is simple: deliver quality technology, build lasting relationships, and serve our community with integrity. Whether you are a county procurement officer, a Fortune 500 IT director, or a growing business owner, Millennium Tech Solutions is your partner from first contact to final delivery.
