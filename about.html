Who We Are

A Technology Partner You Can Count On

Millennium Tech Solutions was founded with a single mission: to deliver enterprise-grade technology solutions with the personal touch of a community-focused partner. With over 20 years of experience in the technology industry — including extensive work across the Gulf region — we have brought that global expertise home to Southern California.

We specialize in IT equipment procurement, hardware solutions, VR/AR technology, and enterprise software. We are proud registered vendors with both Riverside County and Los Angeles County, and we actively pursue vendor and supplier relationships with major corporations throughout SoCal — from pharmaceutical giants to financial institutions to aerospace companies.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Growth & Innovation

2004

Founded with a Vision

Millennium Tech Solutions is established with a focus on delivering premium IT equipment and technology services. Operations begin serving enterprise clients across the Gulf region.

2008–2014

Gulf Region Expansion

Rapid growth across the Gulf, serving government ministries, major corporations, and healthcare systems. Developed deep expertise in large-scale IT procurement and infrastructure deployment.

2016

VR & AR Division Launched

Recognized the transformative potential of immersive technology. Launched dedicated Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality solutions division, serving training, simulation, and enterprise visualization needs.

2020

Expanding to Southern California

Brought our global expertise to SoCal, establishing roots in the Riverside/Inland Empire region. Began building relationships with local government agencies, healthcare systems, and enterprises.

2022

Government Vendor Registration

Successfully registered as an approved vendor/supplier with Riverside County and Los Angeles County for IT equipment, computer parts, and peripherals — a milestone that opened doors to serve the public sector.

Today

Serving All of SoCal

Actively building vendor relationships with major SoCal corporations including pharmaceutical companies, banks, aerospace firms, and healthcare networks — while continuing to serve government agencies and growing businesses.
