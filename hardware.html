Common Questions

Hardware Procurement FAQ

Do you supply to government agencies?

Yes. Millennium Tech Solutions is a registered vendor/supplier with Riverside County and Los Angeles County for IT equipment, computer parts, and peripherals. We understand government procurement requirements and compliance standards.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We serve clients of all sizes — from single-unit orders to enterprise bulk deployments of hundreds or thousands of units. There is no minimum order requirement.

Do you offer hardware leasing?

Yes. We can work with you to explore leasing options that help manage upfront costs while keeping your technology current. Ask us about our leasing and financing options.

How quickly can you deliver?

Delivery timelines vary by product and quantity. Standard in-stock items can typically be delivered within 2–5 business days across Southern California. Custom configurations may take longer.

Do you handle warranty claims?

Absolutely. We manage warranty claims and RMA processes on your behalf, minimizing downtime and administrative burden for your IT team.

Can you help with hardware lifecycle planning?

Yes. We offer strategic hardware lifecycle planning services to help you plan replacements, budget for upgrades, and maximize the return on your technology investments.
